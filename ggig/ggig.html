Get Groceries in Glasgow
About
What
A simple and beautiful website that will help you find places to buy groceries in Glasgow whether you are at school or at home or in the park!

This website aims to help outlanders, for instance, international students, get groceries for cooking.

Contains latest POI (Point of Interest) information for foods and drinks, EXCLUDING delicatessens, restaurants, fast food outlets, etc.

How
For PC & Mac user:
  • Hover on Title to open filter
  • Click filter button to filter your destination
  • Click menu button to open and close side table
  • Double click filter button to generate side table body
  • Click on the table element to fly to it
  • Click on the table header to sort the data by alphabetical order
  • Click symbol in map to generate a discription popup
  • Pan, zoom and rotate the map with the function button on the bottom-right corner. You can also use your mouse wheel and right click.
  • Find your current location with the function button on the bottom-right corner. May require your consent to access location information. Click again to disable the find location function.
For mobile device user:
  • Touch or hold on Title to open filters
  • Touch filter button to filter your destination
  • Touch menu button to:
    • Close filters
    • Open and close side table
    • Close About page
  • Double Touch filter button to generate side table body
  • Touch on the table element to fly to it
  • Touch on the table header to sort the data by alphabetical order
  • Touch symbol in map will generate a discription popup
  • Pan, zoom and rotate the map with the function button on the bottom-right corner. You can also use your touch screen gesture.
  • Find your current location with the function button on the bottom-right corner. May require your consent to access location information. Touch again to disable the find location function.
